How it works for your clinic
Simple setup.
Then it runs itself.

We handle everything. You manage your calendar the same way you always have — LOKKT just brings more patients to it.

Step 01
📋
We set you up together
One of our team sits with you for 30 minutes. We create your profile, set your hours, and walk you through the dashboard. No IT. No disruption. No technical knowledge needed.
Step 02
📅
Set your availability once
Tell us your opening hours and appointment length. LOKKT automatically generates your available slots and shows them to patients. When a slot is booked, it disappears instantly.

Patients book. Reminders go out.
