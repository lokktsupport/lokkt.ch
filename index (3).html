Simple setup.
Then it runs itself.
01
We set you up together
One of our team sits with you for 30 minutes. We connect to your existing calendar — Google, Outlook, or Apple — and your availability appears on LOKKT automatically. No IT. No migration.

Patients book. You see it instantly.
When a patient books through LOKKT, the appointment appears in your existing calendar automatically. When they cancel, the slot reopens. Everything stays in the calendar you already use.
